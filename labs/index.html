Labs

Five hands-on labs that take you from WAF deployment to production-grade hardening — covering every recommendation from the MS WAF best practices page.

Lab Time What you do
Lab 1 — Deploy & Baseline ~30 min Deploy with azd up, verify connectivity, confirm Detection Mode
Lab 2 — Attack & Detect ~20 min Fire SQLi, XSS, path traversal — observe True Positives in WAF logs
Lab 3 — Prevention & Tuning ~40 min Enable Prevention Mode, resolve an O’Brien False Positive with Rule Exclusion
Lab 4 — Custom Rules ~30 min IP block, rate-limit, geo-filter with Custom Rules
Lab 5 — Bot & Observability ~45 min Bot Manager, KQL dashboards, alerting, Sentinel, azd down
